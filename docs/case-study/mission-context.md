# Mission Context

## Purpose

This section defines the operational context in which the system is intended to operate. It establishes the mission objectives, environmental constraints, and external factors that influence system design.

## Mission Overview

The Autonomous Infrastructure Inspection Drone System is designed to perform remote inspection of critical infrastructure, including:

- power lines
- pipelines
- bridges
- industrial facilities

The system enables operators to gather visual and sensor data without direct human exposure to hazardous environments.

## Operational Objectives

The primary objectives of the system are:

- Enable safe and efficient inspection of infrastructure
- Reduce the need for manual inspection
- Provide high-quality data for analysis
- Operate in semi-autonomous or autonomous modes

## Operational Environment

The system operates in:

- Outdoor environments
- Variable weather conditions (wind, temperature, humidity)
- Urban and rural areas
- Areas with potential signal interference

## Constraints

- Regulatory constraints (airspace restrictions)
- Battery limitations
- Communication range limitations
- Safety requirements

## Stakeholder Impact

The mission directly impacts:

- Infrastructure operators
- Maintenance teams
- Regulatory authorities
- Safety personnel

## Engineering Implications

- Need for robust navigation and control
- Reliable communication systems
- High autonomy capability
- Strong safety mechanisms
